Brand guidelines (public)

Protect the Lockard LLC identity with consistent color usage, spacing, and attribution that mirrors how the studio presents itself across survivor-first work.

Core principles

  • Do not distort or recolor the Lockard LLC logo or SafePaws lockup.
  • Maintain generous clear space: at least the height of the “L” on all sides.
  • Pair Lockard LLC with mission-first language; avoid marketing jargon that dilutes survivor trust.

Color palette

Usage Hex Notes
Primary #356BFF Headers, primary call-to-action buttons, and navigation accents.
Secondary #7C5CFF Alternate pill accents, gradients, and SafePaws spot illustrations.
Neutral #0F172A Body copy and accessibility-safe text on light backgrounds.

Typography

Use the Inter family—weights 400, 500, 600, and 700—for all digital and print materials. Avoid substituting with system fonts unless you are preparing a template for localization.
