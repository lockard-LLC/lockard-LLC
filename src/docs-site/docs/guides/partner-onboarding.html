Partner onboarding (public)

Use this checklist to prepare your clinic, shelter, or advocacy team before we activate program access, data sharing, and escalation pathways.

Request Intake Session Review SafePaws scope
Alignment call 45-minute session covering survivor safety, communications, and geography.
Access timeline Most partners receive portal credentials within 3 business days.

Before you reach out

Review the SafePaws overview and confirm your organization can provide trauma-informed care, emergency coordination, and confidential communication channels.

Intake steps

  1. Review the program overview and confirm leadership approval.
  2. Email safepaws@lockard.llc with your capacity, service area, and primary contact.
  3. Join a 45-minute alignment session to outline protocols, escalation paths, and data stewardship expectations.

What happens next

  • We configure secure communications and share the survivor support intake template.
  • Your team receives staff training resources, operating procedures, and 24/7 contact details.
  • Active partners gain access to the SafePaws partner site at safepaws.lockard.llc.

Need help?

Email support@lockard-llc.com for documentation questions or use the contact form on lockard.llc for other business inquiries.
