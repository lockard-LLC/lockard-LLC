SafePaws overview (public)

SafePaws keeps survivors and their animals together through crisis boarding, trauma-informed veterinary care, and coordinated resource navigation grounded in lived experience.

Join the partner network Visit safepaws.lockard.llc
Partners onboarded Shelters, veterinary clinics, and advocacy teams spanning multiple regions.
Coordination windows Rapid-response playbooks help us respond within hours when emergencies arise.

What we do

  • Arrange emergency boarding with trusted partners while keeping pets geographically close.
  • Coordinate veterinary care that honors survivor confidentiality and payment flexibility.
  • Provide rapid resource navigation across housing, legal aid, and financial support.

Who we serve

Survivors of domestic violence who refuse to abandon their animals, along with the clinics, shelters, and advocates who help them rebuild safely.

How to become a partner

  1. Review the partner onboarding guide.
  2. Email safepaws@lockard.llc with your capacity and region.
  3. Complete the alignment session to tailor protocols, training, and escalation paths.

Ready for the full experience? Visit safepaws.lockard.llc for live program materials and secure partner tools.
