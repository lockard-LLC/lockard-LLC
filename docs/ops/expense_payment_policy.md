# Expense & Payment Policy (Pilot)

**Owner:** Jerry • **Visibility:** Internal

**Payments:** clinic invoices **net‑15** post‑reconciliation.  
**Approvals:** VOC ≤ $100; CEO > $100 or > 2 visits/90 days.  
**Reimbursements:** pre‑approval; receipts required; ACH monthly.  
**Documentation:** invoice PDF + ledger entry + email thread reference.  
**Documentation system:** enter math + uploads through `admin.lockard.llc` → Tools → Finance so `data/server` logs the action and audit trail.
**Violations:** hold payment; escalate; log incident in portal + `docs/ops/incident_response.md`.
