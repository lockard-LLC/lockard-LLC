## PetPass × Clinic — Micro‑MOU (Pilot)

> **Purpose.** Establish a simple, time‑boxed pilot between **Lockard LLC (PetPass)** and **[Clinic Name]** to provide discounted basic veterinary care for community members using Case IDs instead of names.

## 1) Term & Scope

* **Term:** 6 weeks from countersignature. Either party may end the pilot on 7 days’ written notice.
* **Scope (Covered):** initial exam, basic diagnostics, essential medications, urgent visit fee.
* **Pre‑Approval Required:** imaging (X‑ray/ultrasound), surgery/hospitalization, specialty referral, or any single visit expected to exceed the voucher cap.

## 2) Scheduling

* Clinic reserves **1–2 “PetPass slots” per week** during regular hours. Specific days/times may be adjusted week‑to‑week by mutual email confirmation.

## 3) Discounts & Payment

* **Partner discount:** **30%** off covered services for approved cases.
* **Voucher cap:** PetPass issues a voucher per case showing a **cap (USD)** and **expiry date**.
* **Co‑pay:** Survivor co‑pay (if any) is **$0–$10**, collected by clinic and shown on invoice.
* **Invoicing:** Clinic invoices **Lockard LLC** **net‑15**, including: gross amount, discount %, co‑pay collected, **net due**, **Case ID**, and date of service.
* **Identifiers:** Clinic uses **Case ID only** on all PetPass paperwork; no survivor names included.

## 4) Confidentiality & Privacy

* Clinic will treat PetPass cases discreetly (neutral front‑desk handling; no public announcements).
* Staff will use neutral language in communications (avoid “DV/abuse” wording).
* Clinic will not include survivor names or contact details in invoices or emails to PetPass.

## 5) Communications

* Primary contact at clinic: **[Name / Role / Email / Phone]**.
* Primary contact at PetPass: **Shawn Kelly (Vet Outreach Coordinator)** — [skelly@lockard.llc](mailto:skelly@lockard.llc) / [skelly@petpass.help](mailto:skelly@petpass.help).
* Scheduling/confirmations happen via email/phone with neutral subject lines (e.g., “Community visit — Case PFS‑LEX‑YYMM‑####”).

## 6) Quality & Safety

* Clinic remains responsible for medical judgments and care.
* If a safety or confidentiality concern arises, clinic pauses related communications and notifies PetPass immediately.

## 7) Reporting & Feedback

* Clinic provides brief weekly feedback on slot usage and issues.
* At pilot end, both parties hold a 15‑minute retrospective to assess continuation or adjustment.

## 8) Independent Parties

* Parties are independent contractors; this MOU does not create employment, partnership, or agency.

## 9) Insurance & Licensure

* Clinic represents it maintains customary professional licensure and liability coverage for its services and staff.
