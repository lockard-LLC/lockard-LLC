# Invoicing Guide & Example (Partners)

**Owner:** Shawn • **Visibility:** Partner‑only

**Invoice must include:** Case ID; date of service; brief notes; gross; discount %; co‑pay; **net due**.  
**Math:** net = gross × (1 − discount) − co‑pay (min 0).  
**Terms:** net‑15; PDF to <billing@lockard.llc>.

**Example:**  
Gross $200 • Discount 30% → $140 • Co‑pay $10 → **Net due $130**.  
Sample lines:  

- Service: Initial exam — $80  
- Diagnostics: Basic panel — $120  
- Subtotal: $200  
- Partner discount (30%): −$60  
- Co‑pay collected: −$10  
- **Net due to Lockard LLC:** **$130**  
**Reference:** Case PFS‑LEX‑2511‑0001; DOS 2025‑11‑12.
