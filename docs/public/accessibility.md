## Accessibility Statement

Lockard LLC is committed to making **petpass.help**, **lockard.llc**, and **admin.lockard.llc** accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet **WCAG 2.2 AA** guidelines.

## Our Commitment

* Semantic HTML and proper labeling for forms and controls.
* Keyboard operability for interactive components.
* Sufficient color contrast and focus indicators.
* Captions/alt text for media and images.
* Reduced motion options where applicable (all animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion` per our shared motion tokens).

## Testing & Improvements

We test with automated checks and manual review using current versions of major browsers and assistive technologies. Accessibility is an ongoing effort; we welcome feedback.
